# MIP Module & Assessment Summary

## MIP Module (50 words)

MGTK746 Management in Practice is a 9-week assessment module following completion of 3 Executive Project Weeks (EPWs). Students critically reflect on their EPW experiences, evaluating application of management tools/theories, data synthesis abilities, and collaborative performance. Module runs concurrently with other taught modules. Formative submissions occur in weeks 3 and 7; final assessments due week 9.

## Tasks A1 & A2 (50 words)

**A1 (70%):** 2,000-word individual report critically evaluating EPW contributions and management practice implications. Structure: introduction (100 words), critical reflection on tools/theories, data sources, and collaboration (500 words each), Personal Development Plan with SMART actions (200-400 words).

**A2 (30%):** Portfolio of Evidence. 10 slides plus cover, 1,000-word slide notes. Evidence includes contributions to deliverables, leadership roles, facilitator/peer feedback, critical incidents, and synchronous/asynchronous sessions. Typically 3 slides per EPW, 4 items per slide, 100 words notes per slide.
