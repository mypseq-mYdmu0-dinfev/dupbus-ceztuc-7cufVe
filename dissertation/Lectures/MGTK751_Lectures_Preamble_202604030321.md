# MGTK751 Lectures — Preamble Only

## Preamble

- The following files contains all lecturecasts, each week might have multiple lectures e.g. "Week 1.2 Business Research and Ethics" is the 2nd lecture of week 1
- Text in all caps followed by title-case text items in following lines are likely section heading
  - e.g. In Week 1.2 lecture's overview page, "PHILOSOPHY" is the heading of section encompassing "What is Philosophy?", "Two main stances", "What is expected of you?", etc.
- Each lecture begins with an overview page with the 1st line after week number being "Course overview" (meaningless) and 2nd line after week number being the title of that lecture e.g. "## Week 1.3<br>Course overview<br>Forming a Research Aim and Objectives"
- Each page in a lecture (exc. overview) is denoted by "lesson", e.g. Lesson 1 of 5 = page 1 of 5 of that lecture
- Each page (except. overview) starts with placeholder lines inc. but not limited to:
  - "Top of page"
  - "SKIP TO LESSON"
  - last page's no. & title (e.g. "2 of 5 — What is our ask over the next weeks?") or "Home" for P.1
  - "Lesson content"
- Right after "Lesson content" would be current page's title followed by page no. in next line
  - e.g. On 3rd lesson (page) of Week 1.1 lecture:
```
Top of page [meaningless]
SKIP TO LESSON [meaningless]
2 of 5 — What is our ask over the next weeks? [last page]
Lesson content [meaningless]
Module outcomes [current page title]
LESSON 3 OF 5 [current page no.]
Listed in the tab you can find ... [content starts]
```

- Videos / audios all come with transcripts, which would be placed right below its file name and size (e.g. Transcript-Module Overview.pdf<br>127.8 KB<br>Module Overview<br>Throughout the course, we will cover ...), where a transcript always ends with a line with just a single number, indicating transcript's end (e.g. ... So, you are in control of this process.<br>2)
- At the end of every page is next page's number & title, followed by a line with single dot `.` added by me as minor separator
  - e.g. at the end of P1 of Week 1.1: "... Feedback given in break out rooms<br>2 of 5 — What is our ask over the next weeks?"
- At the end of every lecture is 3 lines of single dots `.` added by me as major separator
- When you see the text "bullet" followed by text in the immediate next line, it is a bullet pt. And if you see that line of text was followed by a blank line and then line(s) of content, those are sub-bullet pts under that bullet pt

```
e.g.
bullet [denote bullet pt]
Traditional Western view is creativity:  [bullet point content]

Belonging to gifted few [sub-bullet point 1]
Associated with radical new ideas [sub-bullet point 1]
```

- Table contents are converted to csv style enclosed by <csv></csv>
- Images are converted to pure text enclosed by <image_descr></image_descr>
- Heavy content (e.g. YouTube video) processed by Gemini are enclosed by <gemini></gemini>
- Some lectures might end with a reference page/file (e.g. week 1 ends with `References-Part1.pdf`), but don't trust them by 100%. Some of them might have dead links, typos, or simply not in the required citation format (Harvard style).
