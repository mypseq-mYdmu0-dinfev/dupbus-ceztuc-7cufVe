## ⚠️ Ethics —— Critical Constraints (topic-selection impact)

### What requires ethical approval
Questionnaire, survey, focus group, observation, tests, secondary data with personal info

### Hard exclusions (cannot do these → affects topic choice)
- Any vulnerable participant: children (<18), those unable to consent, those unable to refuse (economic/social coercion)
- Deception of participants
- Sensitive topics: bereavement, criminal activity, embarrassing/personal subjects
- Studies causing discomfort/anxiety/stress
- Anything requiring LOREC review → **change topic** (takes too long)
- Research on people over whom researcher has authority (e.g. own direct reports)

### Process (Wks 7`~`11) —— indicative, iterative
1. **Wk7:** Proposal approved by supervisor + second reviewer (2 loops possible)
2. **Wk8:** If in-company/institutional consent needed → obtain separately (can delay; no extension allowed)
3. **Wk9:** Ethics application approved by supervisor
4. **Wk11:** Ethics application approved by second reviewer → written confirmation → **only then can data collection begin**

> ⚠️ **LOREC referral = topic change signal.** Avoid topics that could trigger LOREC (high-risk).

### Transcription constraint
**Only permitted:** MS Teams, MS Office, MS Copilot
Zoom or any other platform = breach of research integrity

### Data storage
Must comply with GDPR; anonymise all participant data; survey invites must use BCC
