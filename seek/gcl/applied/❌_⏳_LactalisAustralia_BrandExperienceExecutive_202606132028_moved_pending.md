## 1. Employer

**Lactalis Australia** is the local arm of the **Lactalis Group**, the world's #1 dairy producer. Founded in 1933 by André Besnier in Laval, France, and still privately owned by the Besnier family, the group employs 85,000⁺ people across 90⁺ countries with 250⁺ production sites globally. In Australia, Lactalis employs approximately 2,500 people and stewards some of the country's most iconic consumer dairy brands:

- **Pauls** (90⁺ years; milk, cream, yoghurt, custards)
- **Oak** and **Ice Break** (flavoured milk beverages with strong convenience channel presence)
- **Vaalia**, **Tamar Valley Dairy**, **Jalna**, **Lemnos** (yoghurt and specialty dairy)

Key company values: **Ambition, Simplicity, Engagement**. Community programme: **"Dairy for Good"** grants — Lactalis brands (including OAK Plus Protein) sponsor community sporting clubs and initiatives across Australia, directly relevant to the brand activation focus of this role.

**Recent developments (2025–2026):**
- Successfully acquired Fonterra's global consumer and foodservice assets (ACCC-cleared 2025) — significant portfolio expansion
- Closing South Brisbane milk plant (July 2026) and earlier Rockhampton plant, consolidating production into fewer, larger facilities
- AUD 200⁺ million capital expenditure commitment to modernise manufacturing through 2025–26

The role is based in **Lidcombe, Western Sydney** — Lactalis AU's operational hub. Organisationally a marketing executive/manager-level position within the Beverages and Milk division, focused on the **Convenience and Independent supermarket channels**. Part-time 23 hrs/wk (4/5 days preferred).
