## Gaps Found (addressed in applied changes below)

1. **S0.3 conflict with sub-agent mode:** `ajap.md § S0.3` still instructs SA to print `🎯[N]` to user. Change 01 adds a Chat Rule note but S0.3 itself overrides it by mandate. Fix: add sub-agent caveat directly to S0.3 (Change 10, new).

2. **S6.4.7 same conflict:** "MUST print cumulative count" in S6.4.7 would cause SA to output C2 to user. Fix: incorporated into updated Change 05 with explicit sub-agent caveat on that line.

3. **K6 collision when re-processing a CL-compromised job:** if old SA follows S6.2.3.2 (mark Pending, move AR to `/pending/`), new SA's K6 check finds the AR in `/pending/` and silently skips — job never re-processed. Fix: on CL-compromise stop, SA voids the AR (`❌_` prefix per Void Rule) and closes tabs instead of following S6.2.3.2. New SA picks up via Pre-Flight (Tab 2 only, no active AR → Refresh Tab 2 → S2). Change 04 updated accordingly; `main_ajap.md` updated to instruct this.

4. **Pre-Flight rows for `/skipped/` or `/pending/` ARs missing** (identified in `response_202605231736.md` pt 3.1, never applied): SA could re-process an already-decided job after compaction. Fix: Change 11 (new) adds two rows to the Pre-Flight table.

5. **F6 (orphaned AR in `/applied/`) missing from Pre-Flight** (identified in `response_202605231736.md` pt 26, never applied): compaction-interrupted prior cycle may leave a stale `⏳_` AR in `/applied/`. Fix: Change 12 (new) adds F6 definition and references it in the Pre-Flight header.

6. **ScheduleWakeup heartbeat context recovery:** when the 5-min heartbeat fires, MA is re-invoked in a potentially fresh context; it must know the SA session name to resume monitoring. Fix: `main_ajap.md` instructs MA to write SA name to `/seek/runtime/ma_state.md` on spawn and read it on heartbeat recovery.

## Accepted Risks

7. **MA cannot interrupt SA mid-loop:** if SA deteriorates mid-card, MA cannot stop it until the current loop's pause point (S6.4.2.5 or loop-end). Damage is bounded to at most one compromised card outcome. Accepted for temp fix.

8. **SA compaction still possible within a single loop:** a long external portal loop could cause SA to compact before reaching the pause point. The tmp file at `/tmp/ajap_last_decision.md` provides decision-state recovery; Pre-Flight provides tab-state recovery. Accepted.
