# Response to query_202605300927.md
*SA S6.4.2.5 stall —— root causes & fixes*

---

1. **Root Cause Analysis**

- 1.1. **RC1 (primary) —— `cc_reminder.md` rescheduled heartbeat at 300s, not 60s.** `main_ajap.md` schedules the initial heartbeat at 60s (per `changes_202605300615.md` Change 02). However, when the heartbeat fires and MA re-reads `cc_reminder.md`, it follows that file's own rescheduling instruction —— which still said `delaySeconds=300`. All subsequent heartbeats therefore ran at 300s intervals. SA's polling window is exactly 300s (20 × 15s checks); with only one heartbeat per window, the timing was too fragile to intervene reliably before SA gave up.

- 1.2. **RC2 (secondary) —— Heartbeat active SA check had no compromised-CL path.** Step 2 only wrote the submit approval when the CL was clean. When "transformative" (a banned word) was found, the heartbeat did nothing —— `ma_msg.md` stayed "Continue", SA exhausted all 20 checks, then stopped idle. MA only received the report after the full 300s timeout, too late to write a correction.

- 1.3. **RC3 (secondary) —— Compromised-CL correction message omitted form re-paste instruction.** Both the Between-Loop Audit and the intended heartbeat correction said "Fix the CL in the AR, then re-report." SA would edit the AR file, but the corrected text was never pushed back into the Tab 3 SEEK application form. On subsequent approval, the old compromised CL would have been submitted.
