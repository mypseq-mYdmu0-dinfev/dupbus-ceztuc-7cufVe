## Task 1. Mini Campaign Brief

**Campaign objective.** Generate qualified enquiries for tax depreciation schedules by correcting a costly misconception, that older or renovated investment properties have nothing left to claim.

**Target audience.** Australian property investors who own older residential investment properties or have renovated recently, alongside first-time investors, supported by a referral push to accountants, mortgage brokers, and buyers’ agents.

**Key message.** Your older or renovated property may still hold valuable depreciation potential, and a qualified Quantity Surveyor can identify what is claimable.

**Recommended channels.** Google Search to capture high-intent investors, Meta for interest-based reach and lookalikes of the existing database, LinkedIn for referral partners, and an end-of-financial-year email to current contacts, with Reddit and investor-forum communities monitored for audience insight.

**Call to action.** See what your property could hold, request your free, no-obligation estimate.

**Key metrics.** Primarily qualified enquiries and cost per qualified lead, measured against Duo Tax’s historic baseline, and secondarily the enquiry-to-schedule conversion rate, tying campaign spend directly to schedules sold.
