# Alltech AV — Research Findings Brief
*Compiled 19 June 2026 by research SA (web). AU-focused. Uncertainty flagged inline. Saved for the Stage 2 deliverable; not for submission.*

## 1. Women in AV / AVIXA Women's Council (ANZ) — named partnership target
- Global professional community under **AVIXA** (Pro AV industry association); open to all, not only women. ([avixa.org](https://www.avixa.org/community/committees-and-councils/avixa-womens-council))
- 2026 activity: IWD + fall + year-end virtual events; Women's Breakfast at InfoComm26 (US); ISE 2026 event.
- **ANZ/Sydney touchpoints (actionable):**
  - **Sydney IWD meetup** — 10 March 2026, Barangaroo (recurring annual ANZ).
  - **AVIXA Women's Council Forum inside Integrate 2026 (2–4 Sept, ICC Sydney)** — the ANZ AV industry's main annual trade show; single biggest local engagement window. ([cxnetwork.com.au](https://www.cxnetwork.com.au/registration-now-open-for-integrate-2026/))
  - New global "Women in AV Collective" launched at ISE 2026 (sponsors: Diversified, Shure).
- **Realistic entry for a boutique:** AVIXA membership + Council on Xchange (low cost); attend/host at the Sydney meetup or Integrate Forum; supply AV/LED **in-kind** (showcases capability to the exact audience); content collaboration. Global sponsorship likely oversized; **local/in-kind is the entry point.** ANZ sponsorship pricing not public (contact AVIXA).

## 2. Sydney/AU corporate-events & AV market 2025–2026 — growing but moderating
- **65%** of event pros plan more events in 2026 (down from **81%** in 2025); **96%** say spend is a priority but only **63%** will increase budgets; cost containment now leading factor. ([spicenews.com.au](https://www.spicenews.com.au/industry-news/report-more-events-on-horizon-in-2026/))
- **AV is the soft spot: only 31% expanded tech budgets** (still "nice-to-have"). Experience/stage production favoured where budgets rise. **AI adoption strong (87% increasing AI investment, 78% already using).**
- Market sizing (directional, vendors vary): AU Pro AV ~USD 93.9M (2025) → 133.6M (2034), ~3.88% CAGR.
- **Calibration:** target steady share-of-voice and pipeline gains, not hockey-stick growth.

## 3. Competitor online benchmark
- **Encore (ex-Staging Connections):** largest APAC; broad corporate site; venue in-house network.
- **Microhire:** strongest content benchmark — **"Insights" hub ~1–2 articles/month**, consistent, SEO-structured; weak on video/multimedia diversity (a gap). ([microhire.com/insights](https://microhire.com/insights/))
- **Boutique Sydney peers** (AVisual, Sodium AV, CMG AV, Eventspec): website + service-page + testimonial driven; **case studies often shallow/portfolio-style, not structured narratives.**
- **Opening for Alltech:** genuine structured case studies (challenge→solution→result), short-form video, consistent social cadence — exactly where peers are weak and Culous is strong.

## 4. AI tooling for content & video, mid-2026 (one part-time marketer)
- **Video now viable solo:** Google **Veo 3.1** (4K, ~60s, native audio); **Runway Gen-4** (brand/character consistency, marketer-focused edits); **InVideo** (high-volume social, pipes in Sora/Veo). ~AUD 60–230 per shippable clip vs $400–2,000 traditional.
- **Copy/case studies:** Claude / ChatGPT for drafting + multi-format repurposing of one event.
- **Honest limits:** AI suits B-roll/atmosphere/titles and first drafts, **not** authentic client-event proof (the AV sector's real selling asset); brand QA, factual accuracy, and client approval remain manual. AI accelerates **volume**, not **judgement**.

## 5. Cadence & organic-traction benchmarks (1 person, ~24hr/wk)
- Sustainable: **~3–5 social posts/week (LinkedIn-led) + 1–2 case studies/month** (matches Microhire's proven cadence). Consistency beats volume.
- Organic, no-paid traction: **60–90 days** for momentum; **3–6 months** for meaningful inbound/ROI; LinkedIn compounds. Niche ICP content far outperforms generic. A content-only, no-paid, no-outbound programme should expect the **slower end (3–6 months).**

**Cross-cutting signal:** market rewards experiential/LED but is budget-cautious on AV-as-line-item; peers weak on structured case studies + video; AI makes solo video feasible; honest organic timelines are 3–6 months. Credible plan = disciplined, AV-native, video-forward content at ~3–5 posts/week + 1–2 case studies/month, anchored locally via the Integrate 2026 Women's Council Forum.
