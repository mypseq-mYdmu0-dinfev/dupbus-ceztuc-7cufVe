# Cheat Sheet — Attachments & Provenance (Alltech)
*So you can say "yes, I read the pack" and "here is where that came from". Skim, do not memorise.*

## The 5 attachments (one line each)
- **Prep Brief** — the task + the rubric (Realism, Judgement, Commercial, Builder, Constraints, Communication, Ego-re-gates); AI encouraged; deliverable due 9am.
- **Company Background** — verticals in priority order (#1 Corporate Conferences, #2 Brand Activations, #3 B2B Production partnerships); values = GROW EPIC; culture = "high-functioning sports team"; runs on EOS; tools = Teamwork, Claude.
- **EOS Role Blueprint** — the role: reports to Elena (Seb = final say + budget); the 6 pillars; the aggressive KPI dates you reworked (20 case studies by Dec, HubSpot + Rentman by Nov, website by 2027); Rentman is named here.
- **Onboarding Plan (draft)** — the baseline you reworked; anything marked "(kept)" is theirs.
- **Benefits & Perks** — staged perks; day-one = provided computer + travel reimbursement; mostly hedged. Least relevant.

## "Where did you get this?"
- **Market stats (65 / 81 / 31%)** — 2026 Cvent ANZ Event Industry Report (via Spice News). A named source, not a guess.
- **Women in AV = AVIXA Women's Council / Integrate** — your research, on top of their "Women in AV Australia".
- **Realistic numbers (3–5 posts/wk, 1–2 case studies/mo, 20 → 10–12, 3–6 month traction)** — industry benchmarks + your judgement.
- **AI tooling (Veo/Runway, the agentic system)** — your research + your own build experience.
- **Verticals, pillars, KPI dates, EOS, team names, Rentman, HubSpot, constraints** — all their documents.
- **The reshaping itself (front-loading, phasing, sequencing, hour-budgets)** — your judgement. This is what they are testing.
