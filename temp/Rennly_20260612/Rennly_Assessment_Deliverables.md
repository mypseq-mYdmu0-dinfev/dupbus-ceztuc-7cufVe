## The Brief

The task was to create an Instagram Reel promoting a premium apartment development in Sydney, with the objective of generating enquiries from buyers and investors. AURÉA is a fictional waterfront development at Rhodes, created to showcase how the right story, language, and craft can turn a property launch into a stream of qualified leads.

## The Concept

The name AURÉA draws on the Latin word for gold, which sets the creative direction. Every frame sits in a warm amber and golden hour palette on deep, moody tones, a treatment that speaks to premium property and aligns with the HOME789 visual identity. The Reel sells a feeling first and the specifications second, opening on an emotive hook and closing on a single, clear action.

Because Chinese and Korean speaking buyers are a primary market, the Reel carries an elegant line of Traditional Chinese alongside the English, a deliberate signal of cultural fluency rather than a translation for its own sake.

## Reel Storyboard, 30 seconds, 1080 x 1920

1. 0 to 5s, Hook. The line "Wake up to this" lands within the first three seconds over a dusk Sydney skyline, then resolves into the AURÉA wordmark and a "Rhodes Waterfront" kicker.
2. 5 to 9.5s, Living. "Light that follows the day", over a golden hour living room framed by floor to ceiling glass.
3. 9.5 to 14s, Kitchen. "Crafted for the way you live", over a stone island kitchen.
4. 14 to 18.5s, Bedroom. "Wake to the harbour", over a sunlit master bedroom.
5. 18.5 to 23.5s, Amenity. "Rooftop infinity pool" and "A sky lounge for residents", over the rooftop at dusk.
6. 23.5 to 30s, Specifications and call to action. Two and three bedroom residences, priced from $1.2M, launching Spring 2026, a golden "Register your interest" button, the website, and the Traditional Chinese tagline.

Slow camera movement on each scene, animated text, and soft crossfades carry the eye through the sequence whilst the palette holds it together.

### 4. Suggested Call to Action

Primary, used on screen and in the caption. "Register your interest today at AUREA-Rhodes.com.au".

Expanded, for the link in bio or a story sticker. "Register at AUREA-Rhodes.com.au to receive the full price list, floorplans, and a private appointment at the AURÉA display suite." A single, specific next step with a clear reason to act will convert far better than a general invitation to direct message.

### 5. Suggested Music and Audio

A warm, cinematic downtempo or melodic deep house instrumental, around 100 to 110 BPM, soft piano over a slow building sub bass with a gentle beat entering at the interior scenes, and no vocals so the captions lead. The level stays subtle, with a soft swell landing on the final call to action.

Useful Canva Audio search terms are "luxury cinematic", "inspiring ambient piano", and "deep house chill". On Instagram, selecting a track from the trending audio library at upload also extends organic reach.

## How It Meets the Brief

- Hook within the first three seconds, an emotive line over a striking skyline.
- Motion graphics and animated text on every scene.
- Captions throughout, suitable for sound off viewing.
- Clear crossfade transitions and a consistent premium palette.
- A strong, specific call to action with a destination.
- Bilingual copy that speaks to the core buyer market.
