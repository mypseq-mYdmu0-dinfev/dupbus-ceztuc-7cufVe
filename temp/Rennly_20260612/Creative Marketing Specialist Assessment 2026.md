## DESIGN TASK

### Scenario

You have been asked to create an Instagram Reel promoting a premium apartment development in Sydney.

The objective is to generate enquiries from potential buyers and investors.

### 01. Deliverables

Please provide:
- Final Reel (MP4)
- Canva link / Design File
- Caption copy
- Suggested CTA
- Suggested music/audio

### 02. Creative Requirements

The Reel must include:
- Hook within first 3 seconds
- Motion graphics or text animation
- Captions/subtitles
- Clear scene transitions
- Strong call-to-action
- Premium visual presentation
- Instagram Reel format (1080 × 1920)

### 03. Assessment Criteria

We will assess:
- Creative thinking
- Visual design quality
- Video editing skills
- Copywriting
- Storytelling
- Social media best practice
- Attention to detail
- Ability to work independently

### 04. Time Limit

20–30 Minutes *(this constraint applies only to the live in-interview setting; pre-preparation has no time limit — produce the best possible work)*

*Stock footage, Canva assets, placeholder renders, and AI-generated imagery may be used.*
