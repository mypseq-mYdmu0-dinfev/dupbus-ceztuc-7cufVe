# Creative Marketing Specialist Assessment

## Instagram Reel — AURÉA, Rhodes Waterfront Residences

Prepared by Culous Yu for Rennly Pty Ltd (HOME789)
Scenario brief: a premium Sydney apartment development created to generate enquiries from buyers and investors.

---

## 1. The Reel (MP4)

**File:** `AUREA_Rhodes_Reel_v2.mp4` (v1 retained for record as `AUREA_Rhodes_Reel_v1.mp4`)
**Format:** 1080 x 1920, vertical Instagram Reel, 120fps, 30 seconds, with audio track.

**Concept.** A fictional luxury waterfront development named AURÉA at Rhodes. The name draws on the Latin "aurum" for gold, echoing the warm amber and golden hour palette that runs through every frame and ties directly to the HOME789 brand direction.

**Storyboard.**
1. 0 to 5s, Hook. A bold "WAKE UP TO THIS" lands over a dusk Sydney skyline within the first three seconds, then resolves into the AURÉA wordmark with a white "Rhodes Waterfront" kicker.
2. 5 to 9.5s, Living. "Light that follows the day" over a golden hour living room.
3. 9.5 to 14s, Kitchen. "Crafted for the way you live".
4. 14 to 18.5s, Bedroom. "Wake to the harbour".
5. 18.5 to 23.5s, Amenity. "Rooftop infinity pool" and "A sky lounge for residents".
6. 23.5 to 30s, Specs and call to action. Two and three bedroom residences, priced from $1.2M, launching Spring 2026, a golden "Register your interest" button, the website, and a Traditional Chinese tagline.

**Creative requirements met.** Hook within three seconds, motion graphics and animated text, captions throughout, crossfade scene transitions, a strong golden call to action, and a premium golden hour treatment held consistently across the whole sequence.

## 4. Suggested Call to Action

Primary, on screen and in caption: "Register your interest today at AUREA-Rhodes.com.au".

Expanded for the link in bio or story sticker: "Register at AUREA-Rhodes.com.au to receive the full price list, floorplans and a private appointment at the AURÉA display suite." This gives a single, specific next step and a clear reason to act, which lifts enquiry conversion against a vague "DM us".

---

## 5. Suggested Music / Audio

A warm, cinematic downtempo or melodic deep house instrumental, roughly 100 to 110 BPM, soft piano over a slow building sub bass with a gentle beat entering around the interior scenes, no vocals so the captions lead. Keep the level subtle and let a soft swell land on the final call to action.

In Canva Audio, useful search terms are "luxury cinematic", "inspiring ambient piano", "deep house chill" and "real estate background". On Instagram, choosing a track from the trending audio library at upload also helps reach, so the supplied MP4 is mixed clean and ready for music to be layered on the platform.
