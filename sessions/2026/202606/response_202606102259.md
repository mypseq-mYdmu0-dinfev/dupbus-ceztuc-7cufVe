2. The condition example has a logic gap, however. "SA pre-CIC context < SA post-CIC return" compares two halves of the SA path against each other — but to determine whether spawning SA *saves context*, the comparison must be between the two alternative routes:
- 2.1. SA route total addition to main session = [SA briefing] + [SA condensed return]
- 2.2. Direct CIC addition to main session = [raw CIC output]
- 2.3. Spawn SA when 2.1 < 2.2; i.e. `[SA briefing + SA return to main] < [direct CIC output to main]`
