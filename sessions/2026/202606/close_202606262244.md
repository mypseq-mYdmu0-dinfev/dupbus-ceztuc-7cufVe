User's chosen workflow: risk-free throwaway creds stored in `cic_bot.md`; at a login wall CC PRINTS the credential in chat for the user to paste (CC never types it). Relay-to-owner of a risk-free cred is fine; entering it is not.

- 2.8. numbered.md —— never put a level-1 number ON a heading (`## 1`) —— it collapses sub-points onto one line; use `##` LABELS with `N.`/`- N.N.` underneath. NEVER reset numbering (continue n+1). Rule now codified in numbered.md.
