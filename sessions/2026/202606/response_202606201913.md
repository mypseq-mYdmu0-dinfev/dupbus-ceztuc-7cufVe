- 10.3. So AJAP's "Tab 1 Accessibility Check" (A1–A6: blank tab → wait 10s → re-check → 3 cycles) IS a bounded poll-wait. That's the term to use henceforth.
- 10.4. Contrast for your mental model: raw `sleep` = the naive, unreliable wait; the MA heartbeat (Monitor file-watch) = the *event-driven* complement; the bounded poll-wait = the *active, expensive, reliable* one. The new file tells SAs to use the latter (or stay productively busy), never raw `sleep`.
